Food Box Locations


At Eco-Eats, we're committed to giving back to the community. We provide food boxes filled with fresh and nutritious ingredients to various locations across the city. These food boxes are available for anyone in need, ensuring that everyone has access to wholesome meals. By donating these food boxes, we strive to promote food security and support our community members in need.
