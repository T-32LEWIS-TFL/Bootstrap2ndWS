About Eco-Eats

Eco-Eats is a leading restaurant chain committed to serving sustainable and delicious food to our customers. Here at Eco-Eats, we believe in sourcing our ingredients responsibly, minimizing our environmental footprint, and providing an exceptional dining experience.

A beacon of hope in the heart of our city. Founded with a passion for combatting food waste and nourishing our community, our establishment is more than just a place to dine - it's a movement towards a more sustainable future. We have embarked on a mission to tackle the alarming issue of food waste generated by local supermarkets and distributors. Through a nonprofit organization, we have established a unique concept: a "Pay As You Feel" restaurant and caf√©. At our restaurant, every meal is a testament to our commitment to sustainability. Our daily changing menu features fresh, diverted food rescued from the brink of landfill, transforming surplus ingredients into delectable dishes bursting with flavor. Whether you're able to pay or not, everyone is welcome to enjoy a nourishing meal crafted with care and dedication.
